Fish Cook Alpha Demo

This is a prototype to demonstrate the basic functionality of the single player and local multiplayer modes of the Fish Cook board game app. It does not contain any bells or whistles, such as sound, new high resolution art or celebrity chefs. The AI is also pretty stupid right now, but it is capable of making the basic decisions to play the game, even if they’re dumb decisions. We will hopefully be able to add all of these things and more with the successful funding of our Kickstarter campaign.

To play local multiplayer, you will need to download and install the free Jovios controller app to your touch screen enabled device, open the app and join the Fish Cook game.
